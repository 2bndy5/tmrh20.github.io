65 # Connection Info
66 
67 Using pin 15/GPIO 22 for CE, pin 24/GPIO8 (CE0) for CSN
68 
69 Can use either RPi CE0 or CE1 pins for radio CSN.
70 Choose any RPi output pin for radio CE pin.
71 
72 **Constructor:**
73 
74  RF24 radio(RPI_V2_GPIO_P1_15,BCM2835_SPI_CS0, BCM2835_SPI_SPEED_8MHZ);
75  or
76  RF24 radio(RPI_V2_GPIO_P1_15,BCM2835_SPI_CS1, BCM2835_SPI_SPEED_8MHZ);
77 
78  RPi B+:
79  RF24 radio(RPI_BPLUS_GPIO_J8_15,RPI_BPLUS_GPIO_J8_24, BCM2835_SPI_SPEED_8MHZ);
80  or
81  RF24 radio(RPI_BPLUS_GPIO_J8_15,RPI_BPLUS_GPIO_J8_26, BCM2835_SPI_SPEED_8MHZ);
82 
83 **Pins:**
84 
85 | PIN | NRF24L01 | RPI | RPi -P1 Connector |
86 |-----|----------|------------|-------------------|
87 | 1 | GND | rpi-gnd | (25) |
88 | 2 | VCC | rpi-3v3 | (17) |
89 | 3 | CE | rpi-gpio22 | (15) |
90 | 4 | CSN | rpi-gpio8 | (24) |
91 | 5 | SCK | rpi-sckl | (23) |
92 | 6 | MOSI | rpi-mosi | (19) |
93 | 7 | MISO | rpi-miso | (21) |
94 | 8 | IRQ | - | - |
95 
96 
97 See http://www.airspayce.com/mikem/bcm2835/index.html for BCM2835 class documentation.
98 Note: The BCM library has been customized slightly to allow use of hardware CE pins not
99 in use for SPI, and to include a millis() function.
